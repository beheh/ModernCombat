Creates great colour effects!

object ScreenRGB(pTarget,dwRGBa,iFadeIn,iDuration,iFadeOut,bAdditive)

ptarget: targetobject (-1 for all)
dwRGBa: color (the alpha value sets the maximum visibility , 0 == 255!!)
iFadeIn: frames to fade in
iDuration: frames to stay idle before fade out (-1 for manual fade out)
iFadeOut: frames to fade out
bAdditive: the effect will be drawed additive


Manual fadeout with:
Fade(iTime)

iTime: frames to fade out (0 == iFadeOut of ScreenRGB())
